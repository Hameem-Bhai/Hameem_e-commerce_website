Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy, bro. No shady business here. 🔒

1. The Information We Collect

To get your custom lighter cases, posters, or digital designs shipped and processed, we need some basic details. This includes your name, email address, delivery address, bKash/Nagad number, and contact info. We only collect details that you explicitly share with us when placing an order or registering an account.

2. How We Use Your Info

We use your info solely to process your orders, ship physical products inside Bangladesh, send receipt emails, and update you on the progress of your digital designs. We do not spam, we do not sell your personal data to random third parties, and we don't do any shady tracking.

3. Security of Your Data

We store order data securely. Payment transactions are handled directly through direct cash on delivery or manually verified bKash/Nagad transfers. We do not store sensitive bank credentials on our servers.

4. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses local storage and cookies to remember what items you put in your cart and keep you logged in to your account. This is strictly functional so that the store actually works properly.
