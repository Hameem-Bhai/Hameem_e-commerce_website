Refund & Return Policy

Let's keep it real. Here's how we handle returns and refunds. 📦🔄

1. Custom Goods (Hand-sculpted Lighter Cases)

Because custom lighter cases are hand-crafted, fully customized to order, and require individual material and time, **we cannot accept refunds or returns for change of mind** once execution starts. Every custom case is unique and made specifically for you.

2. Defective or Damaged Items

If your custom lighter case arrives broken, cracked, or seriously damaged during transit, please contact us **within 24 hours of delivery**. Send us a clear photo/video of the damage on WhatsApp, and we will happily remake and ship a replacement case for you free of charge.

3. Digital Services (Websites, Graphic/Poster Design, Digital Paintings)

For digital services, payments cover consultation, drafting, and programming hours. We do not offer refunds once we start drafting/coding. However, we do offer **unlimited feedback rounds during the drafting phase** to make sure you get exactly what you paid for before the project goes live.

4. Order Cancellations

If you need to cancel a custom order, please notify us within **12 hours** of placing it. Once we buy specific raw materials or start sculpting/coding your order, cancellations are no longer accepted.

5. Need Help?

We want you to be absolutely stoked with your purchase. If you have any issue with your order, message Hameem Bhai directly on WhatsApp, and we'll figure out a way to resolve it together. Hustlers look out for each other. 🤝
