Your Vision, Our Craft

Hameem Bhai er Dokan wasn't built in a boardroom — it was born from the streets of Dhaka out of a simple necessity: people need a place to show their artistic drip without paying astronomical agency fees. Founded by Hameem Bhai, our studio stands for raw creativity.

I'm Hameem Bhai, and this is Hameem Bhai er Dokan — my creative studio in Dhaka, investing my time and late nights so you can flex your creative vision. Whether Hameem Bhai is hand-painting custom 3D lighter cases that catch eyes, coding glassmorphism websites that feel like the future, or Hameem Bhai er Dokan is dropping corporate posters with unmatched aesthetic — we bring that raw, unfiltered passion to everything we touch.

This isn't just a transaction; it's a creative partnership. We iterate, we vibe, and we don't stop until the final product from Hameem Bhai er Dokan is absolute typeshit.
