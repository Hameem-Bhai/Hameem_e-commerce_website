About HameemBhai

The Dokan Story ✨

HameemBhai er Dokan didn’t start in some fancy office. It was born on the streets of Dhaka — from one simple frustration: why tf do people have to pay crazy agency prices just to look good online or flex their creative ideas?

I’m just Hameem Bhai — a regular guy from Dhaka who stays up late coding, designing, and building stuff that actually slaps. Whether it’s glassmorphism websites that feel like the future, fire 3D lighter cases, or clean corporate posters that eat, I pour the same energy into everything.

No gatekeeping. No boring corporate vibes. Just real passion, late-night grinding, and that raw Dhaka hustle. I’m here so you can show your artistic drip without getting rinsed.

This ain’t just business, bro. It’s a whole creative partnership. We vibe, we iterate, we cook until the final product is absolute typeshit. Periodt.

Crafted with love in Dhaka 💖
