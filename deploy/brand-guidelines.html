Official Brand Guidelines
Brand Voice & Personality
"Street soul, not corporate."

Hameem Bhai er Dokan is born from raw Dhaka street culture, coding grinds, and custom hand-sculpted design. Our communication style is friendly, direct, and slightly cheeky. We use local slang ("dokan", "bhai", "slaps") without overdoing it, avoiding sterile or overly professional corporate buzzwords. We sound like a talented creator talking to a friend, not an agency board.

Logo Usage Rules

Do's ✅

  • Use the Circular Logo (transparent PNG) for website favicons, social headers, and circular crops.
  • Use the Horizontal Logo in headers or navigation bars.
  • Use the Monochrome Stamp logo for custom physical prints or receipts.
  • Ensure contrast: use the white monochrome variant on dark backgrounds.

Don'ts ❌

  • Never stretch, squash, or distort the proportions of the mascot face.
  • Do not overlay the dark circular logo directly on dark backgrounds.
  • Avoid adding extra dropshadows, glows, or changing colors of the mascot.
  • Do not change the signature pink accents in the primary circular logo.
