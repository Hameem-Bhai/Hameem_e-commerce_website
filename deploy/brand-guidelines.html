Design Guidelines & Systems

Interactive UI Guidelines

Glassmorphism: Containers must use a transparent dark surface (`rgba(36, 36, 36, 0.6)`) layered with a subtle white border (`rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.08)`) and a heavy backdrop blur (`16px`).

Hover States: Interactive elements should lift slightly (`transform: translateY(-2px)`) and show a soft pink shadow glow (`0 0 20px rgba(244, 168, 199, 0.25)`) using a standard 250ms transition.

Spacing & Layout constants

Layout elements follow a strict 4px grid system: --sp-1: 4px | --sp-2: 8px | --sp-3: 12px
--sp-4: 16px | --sp-6: 24px | --sp-8: 32px
--sp-10: 40px | --sp-12: 48px | --sp-16: 64px
Page clearance margins should always align to standard variables (`--container-pad` at 24px).

Logo Rules

Do's ✅

  • Use the Circular Logo for tab favicons, search results, and social headers.
  • Use the Horizontal Logo in headers or navbar elements.
  • Use the Monochrome Stamp version for physical receipts, packaging, and custom printing.
  • Always keep the logo centered and clear of clutter.

Don'ts ❌

  • Never stretch, distort, or squash the dimensions of the mascot logo.
  • Avoid adding heavy dropshadows or changing color properties of the mascot.
  • Do not overlay the dark logo directly on busy or dark backgrounds.
  • Do not modify signature pink streaks in the mascot's hair.
